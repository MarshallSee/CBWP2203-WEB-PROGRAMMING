About the Career Fair

Our institution is dedicated to promoting student employability and fostering strong links with industry. The annual career fair is one of our flagship initiatives, offering a platform where students and employers connect.

Since its inception, the fair has helped hundreds of participants secure internships and full-time roles. Many of our alumni have started their careers here, building lasting partnerships with the companies they met during the event.

We continue to expand the fair each year, welcoming new organisations and showcasing success stories that highlight the event's impact on our community.
