Welcome to our Virtual Career Fair! This online event connects students, recent graduates, and job seekers with top employers from diverse industries. Join us to explore opportunities and learn how the institution supports your career journey.

Event Overview

The fair aims to enhance graduate employability and strengthen industry-academic collaboration. Participants can meet recruiters, attend live webinars, and access resources on resume writing, job search strategies, and industry insights.

  • Students, fresh graduates, and job seekers are welcome.
  • Build professional networks with potential employers.
  • Gain valuable tips and insights for your career.

Important Dates & Updates

Registration closes: 30 June 2023 • Live sessions begin: 10 July 2023.
